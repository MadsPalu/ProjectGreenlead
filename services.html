01. Hydropower Investment in Greenland

INVESTOR GUIDANCE | TECHNICAL-FINANCIAL ANALYSIS | NAVIGATING THE POLITICAL ENVIRONMENT

We can help your company find the right hydropower project, facilitate or design an offtake industry of the energy and ensure financial viability and sustainability of your project. ​
​
Greenland is in many ways full of fantastic economic opportunities.
​
In Greenland, 66 different hydropower potentials have been cataloged on the Greenlandic west coast, with hydrological data from the 70s. We have a great deal of knowledge of these potentials, as well as the many conditions that one should become familiar with in relation to geography and geology, glacial influence, submarine cables and transmission, population proximity, sales opportunities, local competences for operation and side industries, and much more. ​
​
GreenLead has extensive knowledge of the political process that precedes exploration permits and exploitation permits, as well as how the dialogue around Impact Benefit Analysis (IBA) is approached, so that both Greenland and the Investor achieve the greatest possible success.
​
One of the reasons we know so much is: we have established businesses and been citizens in Greenland for several years from 2012 - 2021, whereby we gained a great deal of knowledge of the business environment, the culture, local contacts, logistics, ice situations, the Greenland terrain and much more which is absolutely essential for running a business in Greenland.
​
For the past 5 years, the team behind GreenLead have worked to realize hydropower investments in Greenland. This has resulted in the political approval and funding of 4 hydropower plants in the Greenlandic parliament from 0.2 MW to 55 MW in effect size - an infrastructure package of approx. 3.1 billion DKK.

02. Heating Batteries

FOR PROCESS PLANTS AND HEATING PLANTS

In other words electrification of gas and oil, via magnesium stone in a heating battery solution.

If your company needs to change from gas and oil to an electric solution, we can help you. We offer turnkey solutions, with changes to process plants and heating centrals which are connected to a heating battery where the heart consists of magnesium stone.

The plant is ideal for both process heat up to 700 degrees, as well as central heating systems in companies or district heating networks for utility companies. The plant can be dimensioned to take over all heat supply, or function as a supplement to other solutions. ​

The efficiency with standard insulation is 95% return of energy, with a daily loss of about 1%. With greater insulation, the loss can be further reduced. ​

The profitability of the plants is quite good, and most often you will experience a cheaper energy solution than gas or oil-based energy. Before each project, a profitability analysis is carried out, so that you know the economic conditions, as well as the prices of gas, oil, and electricity, which are the basis for positive profitability of the plant. ​

GreenLead can also help with leasing arrangements for the facilities, as well as a service and maintenance agreement with guaranteed uptime.

04. Project Development for green energy transition

ECONOMIC ANALYSIS AND PROJECT DEVELOPMENT OF SUSTAINABLE ENERGY SOLUTIONS

If you have a project with energy and sustainability, we are happy to help with project development and economic and technical analysis. We are passionate about the green transition! It gives purpose and meaning to work so that our society, cities, and companies become zero-emission, and our nature is looked after in a way that we can proudly pass it on to our children.

At GreenLead, we have decades of experience with green energy transformation, huge energy systems, and the political work to get projects from ideas to reality.

We have worked with:

  • Hydropower plants in the Arctic from 0.25 MW to 50 MW to 1500 MW plants.
  • Wind turbine industry with small niche turbines down to household needs, up to large offshore wind turbines in the 8-9 MW class.
  • Solar cell systems for the supply of small deserted communities, to slightly larger systems in regular cities.
  • Battery solutions for grid balancing, for storing energy for the night, and for optimizing the ICE GEN set, so that the lifespan is extended and the environment and wallet are saved from burning diesel.
  • Project development for Power to X, E-Fuels, and sector coupling with industrial hubs, where energy producers and several energy-intensive consumers are thought together about their respective needs and by-products.
  • Urban systems for the electrification of ordinary passenger cars, and more recently, the next generation of electrification of transport, where heavy transport such as trucks, buses, and construction machinery also joins the green transition.

Working with these kinds of subjects often becomes political. Our journey has led us into many dialogues with ministers, heads of departments and heads of departments, mayors, municipal directors, professional consultants, you name it-, in the public system. Our key to success is cooperation, patience, and eyes that can see solutions to every barrier.

05. Analysis & Modelling

NATIONAL ENERGY SYSTEMS | UTILITY SECTOR

GreenLead provides the utility sector consultancy services regarding business analysis, with pricing, connection terms, backup obligations, standby costs, peak load alleviation, segmentation of consumer types, the introduction of new technology in utility, and much more. ​

We have worked to create a national framework for energy supply and energy planning for the whole of Greenland. A huge country the size of half of Europe and with relatively many facilities compared to the size of the population.

We have designed national price systems, national standard terms of supply for electricity, water, and heat, connection conditions, specified requirements concerning backup, and much more. We have drawn up the national plan to convert Greenland's remaining diesel power to renewable energy, where we have been the idea creators and driving force behind the adoption of legislation and financing package of DKK 3.1 billion. DKK for the construction of two new large hydropower plants.
